Frequently Asked Questions

    • If your delivery is delayed, please check the status of your order in the app or website. If the status is not updated or you believe there is an issue, contact our customer support team immediately. We will investigate the delay and provide an update as soon as possible. In some cases, delays might be due to high demand, traffic conditions, or unexpected events.

    • Once an order is placed, changes to the delivery address can only be made if the order has not yet been dispatched. To request a change, contact our support team as soon as possible. If the order is already on its way, you will need to wait until the delivery is completed and then arrange for a return or redelivery.

    • If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer support team immediately. Provide details about the issue, including photos of the damaged item or incorrect product. We will arrange for a replacement or refund based on the nature of the problem. Ensure to report these issues within the timeframe specified in our return policy.

    • You can track your delivery in real-time using our app or website. Simply log in to your account, go to the "Orders" section, and select the order you wish to track. You will see the current status and estimated delivery time. If you encounter any issues with tracking, please reach out to our support team for assistance.

    • We accept various payment methods including credit and debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery (where available). You can choose your preferred payment method during the checkout process. If you encounter any issues with payment, please contact our support team for help.

    • The minimum order amount for delivery may vary depending on your location and the delivery partner. This information is usually provided during the checkout process. If your order does not meet the minimum amount, you may be prompted to add more items or select a different delivery option.
